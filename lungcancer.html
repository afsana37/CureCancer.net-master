Lung Cancer

Lung cancer is the uncontrolled growth of abnormal cells that start off in one or both lungs; usually in the cells that line the air passages. The abnormal cells do not develop into healthy lung tissue, they divide rapidly and form tumors. .

Symptoms of lung cancer are cough,shortness of breath,wheezing,chest pain, andcoughing up blood.

Other symptoms can include shoulder pain, hoarseness, or difficulty swallowing. Symptoms of metastatic disease can include headaches, vision changes, seizures, weakness, or symptoms of stroke. Symptoms common to many cancers include fatigue, unexpected weight loss, and depression.
